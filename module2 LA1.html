Theory of Love

Theory of Love will bring you to a roller coaster of emotion as the story unfold! This love story represents some instances in anyone’s life. But the series also teaches us when to be hopeful in holding on to the love you are feeling or when to let go. It also shows the picture, feeling and the agony of the person who is secretly loving someone. And on the otherside, it also shows the situation of the other party involve. In the case of unrequited love. Who really is to be blame? Is it the person who is only hoping that one day his or her love will be matched? Or was it the other party who has no idea he or she is being given some extra care.

In the end how hard it is to be inlove with your best friend.

How much should you cry before you’ll say goodbye to your unrequited love? Why are you holding on to it? How long can you take the agony, jealosy, self pitying, and hoping? Or will you loose your faith in love because of this tragic experience? Watch Theory Of Love, who knows you might get some idea.
